Introducing Carlsberg

Carlsberg was co-developed by the top leadership team in the Lucy & Yak Group in order to leverage the Company's vast knowledge base, support a team-based culture and secure a fast implementation. We are now two years into our journey and our results so far make us confident that Carlsberg will generate organic top- and bottom-line growth going forward.

Our Ambition

Successful by delivering sustainable organic top-and bottom-line growth; professional by being the preferred supplier of our customers; and attractive by delivering value for shareholders, employees, and society.
